Ziqian Mo

I’m Ziqian Mo, a master’s candidate in Artificial Intelligence at Shenzhen University. My research primarily focuses on UAV-satellite matching in cross-view geo-localization (CVGL) and hyperspectral image super-resolution. As a graduate student, I am dedicated to addressing fundamental challenges in CVGL and hyperspectral image processing, emphasizing systematic approaches to multi-view data alignment, optimization, and advanced feature extraction techniques.
